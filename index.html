About Us

Navigating the Mara

TAHADHARI acts as an offline-first conservation intelligence layer above park infrastructure to monitor and combat critical poaching cycles.

By converting fragmented park metrics into predictive, evidence-led protection paths, we empower local wildlife teams to secure the future of the pride.

OUR SERVICES

Core Analytical Services

Predictive Intelligence

AI-powered risk scoring across 1km grid squares using XGBoost, satellite data, and 3 years of crime logs to forecast 48-hour poaching hotspots.

Commander Dispatch

Real-time OpenStreetMap dashboard with color-coded heatmaps. Commanders click red zones to assign patrols and auto-retrain the model on returned field logs.

Offline Visualization

PWA with Service Worker caching. Rangers preload maps and target grids over base Wi-Fi, then access everything offline in zero-signal bush zones.

Fast-Form Logger

3-tap snare reporting with auto-captured GPS and timestamps. Data stores locally in IndexedDB and syncs automatically when connectivity returns.
